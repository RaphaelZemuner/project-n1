Yoga/Mindfulness

Yoga and mindfulness are powerful practices that bring balance to both body and mind. Yoga combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to enhance strength, flexibility, and inner peace. Mindfulness, rooted in staying present and aware, complements yoga by helping you focus on the here and now, reducing stress and fostering clarity. Together, they create a holistic approach to well-being, promoting physical health, mental calmness, and a deeper connection to oneself. Whether you're seeking to improve your fitness, manage stress, or find inner peace, yoga and mindfulness offer a path to a more harmonious life.

Yoga Techniques

Bellow you will find a links of Yoga/Mindfulness techniques that combine physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to enhance strength, flexibility, and mental clarity. These practices harmonize the body and mind, promoting overall well-being.

Bridge Pose

Bridge pose, also known as Setu Bandha Sarvangasana in Sanskrit, is a yoga pose that involves lifting your hips and chest off the ground while lying on your back. It's a backbend that can help with flexibility, posture, and strength.

Learn More
Adho Mukha Svanasana

Adho mukha svanasana is a forward bending type of position. It may help stretch the muscles of the legs, hands, calves and inner thighs. This is a rejuvenating pose. It might help strengthen the muscles and improve the blood supply to the brain.

Learn More
Cat Pose

This short sequence can be repeated to build strength in your mindful core and trim belly fat. It will also inspire awareness through the entire body and spine, helping to alleviate back pain and improve overall posture and body awareness.

Learn More
Cow Pose

Cow yoga pose and cat-cow yoga pose provide the following benefits: Increased mobility of the spine, neck, shoulders, and pelvis. Increased awareness of your spine. Increased relaxation from learning to move with your breath.

Learn More
Triangle Pose

Triangle Pose. Triangle, which is a part of many yoga sequences helps build strength in the legs and stretches the hips, spine, chest, shoulders.

Mindfulness Techniques

Bellow you will find a links of Yoga/Mindfulness techniques that combine physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to enhance strength, flexibility, and mental clarity. These practices harmonize the body and mind, promoting overall well-being.

Mindful/Conscious Breathing

Focus on your breath as it naturally flows in and out. Pay attention to the sensation of air entering your nostrils, filling your lungs, and the gentle rise and fall of your chest. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to your breathing.

Learn More
Body Scan Meditation

Lie down or sit comfortably and slowly bring your attention to different parts of your body, starting from your toes and moving up to your head. Notice any sensations, tension, or relaxation in each area without trying to change anything.

Learn More
Mindful eating

When eating, focus fully on the experience. Pay attention to the color, texture, taste, and smell of your food. Chew slowly, savoring each bite, and notice how your body feels as you eat.

Learn More
Walking Meditation

Walk slowly and deliberately, paying attention to each step. Notice the sensation of your feet touching the ground, the movement of your legs, and the rhythm of your breath. Stay aware of your surroundings, sounds, and the feeling of the air on your skin.

Learn More
Loving-Kindness Meditation

Sit comfortably and bring to mind a person you care about. Silently repeat phrases like "May you be happy, may you be healthy, may you be safe, may you live with ease." Gradually expand your wishes to include yourself, others.
